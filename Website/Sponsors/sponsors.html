OUR SPONSORS

We would like to sincerely thank our wonderful sponsors for having such caring and giving hearts. Without their financial support for this team, our season would not be possible.

The ThunderChickens are proud to have so many community partners who celebrate STEM. These young minds eat up all the knowledge they gain throughout the season. Not only do they learn hard skills about robots, manufacturing, programming, CAD and electrical engineering, they learn other soft skills that will be useful for the rest of their lives. Teamwork, time management, public relations, and many others. Here's to the start of a great season. Thank you again for your generous sponsorship. We hope that we will live up to the achievements of previous years.

We invite you to support these community-minded organizations. If you are interested in becoming a sponsor, be sure to contact us at thunderchickenspr@gmail.com


ACADEMIC

Our team consists of students from the 4 high schools in the Utica Community School District. Many of the students are in Math Science and Technology (MST) and the Center for Science and Industry (CSI) programs.
